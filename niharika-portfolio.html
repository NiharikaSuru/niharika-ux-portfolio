About Me

Designer who bridges research, craft, and code

I work at the intersection of research and interaction design — translating business requirements and stakeholder input into journeys that hold up under real-world use. Most of my time is spent moving between user research, wireframing, and high-fidelity prototyping inside enterprise and CRM systems.

Accessibility and design systems are not an afterthought; they're how I keep fast-moving teams consistent. I also bring a working understanding of front-end implementation, which keeps my handoffs realistic and shortens the distance between design and shipped product.
