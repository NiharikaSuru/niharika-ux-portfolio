F2MX

A fan-to-market experience app for NFL fantasy sports where users buy and sell players like stocks, compete in leagues, earn achievements, and manage virtual wallets — achieving 20% reduction in onboarding friction and 15% increase in user engagement.

Overview

F2MX is a fan-to-market experience app built around NFL seasons and games. Users can buy and sell players like stocks, join or create leagues, earn trading achievements (Bargain Bin, Flip Master, League Winner), and manage their virtual wallet economy — all with a sleek dark-themed mobile interface.

This project documents the mobile product design focused on creating intuitive player trading flows, league management, wallet transactions, and gamification features that drive engagement throughout the NFL season.

Problem

Complex Player Trading

Users found buying and selling players confusing, with unclear pricing, wallet balances, and transaction confirmations.

League Management Friction

Creating leagues, inviting members, and tracking standings required too many steps and lacked clear hierarchy.

Wallet Confusion

Users struggled to understand their wallet balance, transaction history, and how buy-ins affected their available funds.

Research

Competitive Analysis

Analyzed fantasy apps like DraftKings, FanDuel, and Sleeper to identify best practices for player trading and league management.

User Behavior Analysis

Studied trading patterns, peak usage times during NFL games, and common user flows to understand engagement drivers.

Pain Point Mapping

Mapped the complete journey from joining a league to making first trade, identifying 7 critical friction points.

Key Findings

  • Buy/sell confusion: Users needed clearer confirmation of trades showing current balance, player cost, and resulting balance
  • League invite friction: Inviting friends to leagues required too many steps — users wanted in-app invites and QR codes
  • Achievement motivation: Users were highly motivated by achievements and badges but couldn't easily track progress
  • Wallet visibility: Transaction history and wallet balance needed to be more prominent and easier to understand
  • Role clarity: Users wanted clearer distinction between player tiers (Rookie, Amateur, Pro, Head Coach)

Design Goals

Simplify Trading

Make buying and selling players intuitive with clear pricing, balance updates, and confirmation flows.

Streamline Leagues

Enable quick league creation and member invites through in-app search and shareable QR codes.

Gamify Experience

Surface achievements, badges, and progress to drive engagement throughout the NFL season.

Wallet Clarity

Make wallet balance and transaction history immediately clear and accessible from any screen.

UX Process

User Flows

Redesigned core user flows for onboarding, discovery, analysis, and trading — reducing average steps by 40%.

Mobile Wireframes

Created mobile-first wireframes optimized for thumb reach, one-handed use, and quick scanning.

Rapid Prototyping

Built interactive prototypes for weekly testing cycles, enabling fast validation and iteration.

Iterative Design

Conducted 4 rounds of design reviews with stakeholders and users, refining based on feedback.

Usability Testing

Ran 8 remote usability sessions focusing on onboarding completion and trading task success.

A/B Validation

Validated key design decisions through A/B testing on onboarding variants and dashboard layouts.

Design Decisions

Buy Player Flow

Designed a clear buy modal showing player info, current balance, cost, and resulting balance with swap suggestions when roster conflicts exist.

Dark Theme UI

Implemented a premium dark theme with accent colors for status indicators (green for credits, red for debits, cyan for actions).

League Invite System

Created dual invite methods: in-app user search with role badges and shareable Link/QR for external invites.

Achievement Badges

Designed market/trading badges (Bargain Bin, Flip Master) and league outcome badges with clear unlock criteria.

Final Solution

Player Marketplace

Buy/sell players with clear pricing, balance preview, and smart swap suggestions when positions conflict.

League Management

Create leagues, invite members by username or QR code, and track standings with role-based permissions.

Wallet & Economy

Admin portal for transaction management with filtering by wallet type, transaction type, and detailed status tracking.

Achievements System

Gamified experience with Market/Trading badges and League Outcome achievements to drive engagement.

Key Learnings

What This Project Taught Me

  • Balance clarity matters: Showing current balance, cost, and resulting balance in buy flows eliminated user anxiety about transactions.
  • Gamification drives retention: Achievement badges and progress tracking kept users engaged throughout the NFL season.
  • Social features need friction-free invites: In-app invites and QR codes dramatically increased league participation over link-only sharing.
  • Dark themes need careful contrast: Status colors (completed/failed) required extra attention to maintain accessibility in dark UI.
