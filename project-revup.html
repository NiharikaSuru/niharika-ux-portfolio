Revup

A comprehensive CRM platform for lead and deal tracking with integrated calendar, task management, notes, meeting scheduling, and Sales Pursuit AI — resulting in 20% efficiency improvement and 15% faster task completion.

Overview

Revup is a comprehensive CRM platform similar to Zoho, designed for sales teams to track leads and deals through the entire pipeline. The platform enables users to create notes, manage tasks, schedule meetings, and access an integrated calendar — all while leveraging Sales Pursuit AI for intelligent insights and automation.

This project documents the UX redesign focused on unifying lead management, deal tracking, and event creation into a cohesive experience that reduces context-switching and improves sales team productivity.

Problem

Fragmented Workflows

Users struggled with fragmented workflows requiring them to jump between multiple screens to complete simple tasks.

Unclear Task Management

Task management lacked clarity — users couldn't easily see priorities, deadlines, or ownership at a glance.

Excessive Navigation

Multiple actions required excessive navigation, with users averaging 7+ clicks to complete routine lead management tasks.

Research

Stakeholder Discussions

Conducted 8 stakeholder interviews with sales managers, account executives, and operations leads to understand business requirements and pain points.

Workflow Analysis

Mapped existing workflows across 5 key user tasks, identifying bottlenecks, redundancies, and opportunities for consolidation.

Journey Mapping

Created detailed journey maps for primary personas, highlighting emotional pain points and moments of friction.

Task Analysis

Performed task analysis on the 10 most frequent user actions, measuring time-on-task and error rates.

Key Findings

  • Navigation overhead: Users spent 40% of their time navigating rather than completing tasks
  • Unclear ownership: Task ownership was unclear, leading to dropped leads and duplicated effort
  • Communication friction: Communication workflows created friction with no centralized history or context
  • Information overload: Dashboards displayed too much information without clear prioritization
  • Mobile gaps: Field sales teams couldn't effectively use the platform on mobile devices

Design Goals

Reduce Complexity

Simplify multi-step workflows into streamlined actions that require fewer clicks and less cognitive load.

Improve Efficiency

Enable users to complete their most important tasks faster through better information architecture.

Increase Visibility

Provide clear pipeline visibility with at-a-glance understanding of priorities and status.

Support Collaboration

Enable seamless handoffs and communication between team members with shared context.

UX Process

Journey Maps

Created comprehensive journey maps for 3 primary personas, identifying 12 key improvement opportunities.

Information Architecture

Restructured the IA based on user mental models, consolidating 23 screens into 14 focused views.

Wireframes

Developed low-fidelity wireframes for all key flows, iterating through 3 rounds of internal review.

Interactive Prototypes

Built high-fidelity Figma prototypes for usability testing with realistic data and interactions.

Usability Validation

Conducted 6 usability sessions with target users, identifying and addressing 8 critical issues.

Design Iteration

Refined designs based on feedback, creating final specifications for development handoff.

Design Decisions

Unified Dashboard

Designed a dashboard showing leads by assignee, lead status counts, and lead source analytics — giving managers instant pipeline visibility.

Integrated Calendar

Built calendar integration allowing users to schedule meetings directly from lead/deal profiles and sync with external calendars.

Quick Event Creation

Enabled one-click creation of notes, tasks, and meetings from any context — reducing time to log activities by 60%.

Sales Pursuit AI

Integrated AI-powered insights for lead scoring, next-best-action recommendations, and automated follow-up reminders.

Final Solution

Lead & Deal Dashboard

A unified dashboard with tabs for Deals and Leads, showing total counts, conversion metrics, leads by assignee chart, and funnel visualization.

Activity Management

Streamlined creation of notes, tasks, and scheduled meetings with context preserved from any lead or deal record.

Calendar Integration

Full calendar module (My Tasks) allowing users to view, create, and manage meetings alongside their daily schedule.

Sales Pursuit AI

AI-powered module providing lead prioritization, deal probability scoring, and intelligent automation suggestions.

Key Learnings

What This Project Taught Me

  • Unified views reduce friction: Combining leads, deals, and calendar in one interface eliminated constant tab-switching and improved focus.
  • AI features need clear value: Sales Pursuit AI adoption increased when we showed concrete time savings rather than abstract "intelligence."
  • Context is king: Keeping notes, tasks, and meetings attached to lead/deal records eliminated the need for users to hunt for information.
  • Quick actions matter: One-click meeting scheduling and note creation drove daily engagement more than any dashboard visualization.
